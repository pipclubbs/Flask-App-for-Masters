About this web app

For my Masters Research Project I decided to experiment with web scraping. Fed up with the difficulty of locating climbing information I knew was online somewhere but couldn't find it, I decided to investigate why the search engines like Google and Bing are not so great at finding information on niche topics, and to see if building a targeted, climbing-specific search engine using web scrapers was possible. This web application is the result of that experiment.
As it stands, it is a protytpe, proof of concept to see if the idea is feasible and to assess what I learn from putting it together.
