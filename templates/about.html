About me

I'm Pip. I'm from north east England, and have a passion for sport. In particular, rowing, ultrarunning, aerial and weight lifting. But when I'm not doing those I can usually be found at a climbing wall or on a big hill or rock somewhere.

After over twenty years working in project management for academic and educational publishing I decided to get back to education myself and do a Masters in Computer Science - a huge change for me!

In July 2023 I started my first tech job as a Software Developer with Proctor & Gamble, and I am very much enjoying the challenge so far.

You can find me on LinkedIn.


About this web app

This web application was developed as part of my Masters Research Project, which was an experiment with web scraping. Fed up with the difficulty of locating climbing information I knew was online somewhere but couldn't find, I decided to investigate why the search engines like Google and Bing are not so great at finding information on niche topics, and to see if building a targeted, climbing-specific search engine using web scrapers was possible. This web application is the result of that experiment.

As it stands, this web application is a proof-of-concept, limited prototype to see if the idea is feasible and to assess what I learn from putting it together.
